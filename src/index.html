About

I’m a passionate Web Designer and Web Developer based in Germany, with a strong background in creating visually compelling and highly functional websites.

My journey in the digital world began with photography, focusing on nightclub and model photography, where I honed my skills in retouching and editing.

This early experience sparked my interest in the broader field of digital design, leading me to pursue further education as a Media Designer.

To deepen my skills and expand my expertise, I completed an intensive bootcamp in Full-Stack Web Development.

This experience allowed me to master the latest technologies and development practices, enabling me to create dynamic, responsive, and user-friendly web applications.

My expertise now lies in blending creative design with cutting-edge development techniques to deliver user-friendly digital experiences.

I have a keen eye for detail, a deep understanding of UX/UI principles, and the technical skills to bring ideas to life across multiple platforms.
